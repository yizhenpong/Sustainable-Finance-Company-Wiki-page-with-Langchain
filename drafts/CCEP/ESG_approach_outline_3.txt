 The Coca-Cola Company's ESG approach includes:
1. Sustainable business practices in core strategy, focusing on people and the environment
2. Prioritizing water security with a focus on water-stressed regions and replenishing 159% of the water used in finished beverages in 2022
3. Commitment to sustainable agriculture
4. Water leadership through innovation (Coca-Cola Creations and Sprite Limelight) and packaging initiatives (World Without Waste)
5. Climate action and people & communities engagement and partnerships.
